🌟 Popular Pakistani Dishes

  • Biryani – A spicy rice dish with meat and potatoes
  • Karahi – Cooked in a wok with tomatoes, spices, and meat
  • Haleem – A thick stew of meat, lentils, and wheat
  • Nihari – A slow-cooked meat stew, usually eaten for breakfast
  • Chapli Kebab – Minced meat patties with herbs and spices
